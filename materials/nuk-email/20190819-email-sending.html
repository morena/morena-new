Nature Sense bottles are closer
to nature than ever before
Our Nature Sense range is as close to mother’s breast as never before. The teat features several tiny openings which mimic mother’s milk ducts as well as a particularly wide lip rest and the NUK Anti-Colic vent. This lets babies enjoy an all-round natural feeling when drinking – almost as if at mother’s breast.
Introducing ourSpace Soother
When your little one starts their journey into their world, their young and delicate skin is particularly sensitive. This is why NUK has designed the new NUK Space Silicone Soother to protect the skin in the best possible way and to ensure the greatest comfort for your newborn at all times.
